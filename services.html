Services



SATOP Assessment


Assessments consist of...
  • Personal interview with a Qualified Substance Abuse Professional
  • Administration of a computerized screening assessment
  • Review of Blood Alcohol Content (BAC)
  • Review of Department of Revenue Driving Record

Cost...

The assessment cost is $375, paid at the time of the assessment. This cost includes $126 for the assessment and $249, called a Supplemental Fee, that is collected by the agency and sent to the Department of Mental Health in Jefferson City. All SATOP costs are standardized throughout the state and are due at the time of services. SATOP Program/Class levels are as follows:



Level I – Offender Education Program (OEP)


  • 10 hour education program.

  • $130


Level II – Weekend Intervention Program (WIP)


  • 20 hour education program.

  • $250 - $467.45, based on your income, number of dependents, etc.


Level III – Clinical Intervention Program (CIP)


  • 50 hours of outpatient counseling.

  • $250 - $1046.91, based on your income, number of dependents, etc.


Level IV – Serious Repeat Offenders Program (SROP)


  • Minimum of 75 hours of outpatient counseling.

  • $250 - $1500; based on your income, number of dependents, etc.
